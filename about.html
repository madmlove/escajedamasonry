Who we are

Welcome to Escajeda Masonry

Escajeda Masonry is a construction based company in Pittsburgh, PA. Since 2006, it has been our privilege to assist residential and commercial clients in resolving their masonry restoration and new construction requests. Through our experience, personable services, and professionalism, we finish your requests correctly and on time.

Whether you're looking to install a new chimney for your home or you want to repair the stone or brickwork at your business, Escajeda Masonry is the company to call. With 16 years of experience, you can count on our quality work. Our team is here to help make your experience with Escajeda Masonry easy and flawless. Check out our core team members below!
